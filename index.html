An app that keeps your eyes healthy.

EyeLeo for Mac is an app that regulary reminds you to take a rest for your eyes, shows you simple eye exercises and prevents you from using Mac during these exercises. When you're working at a computer it's easy to forget about time. This leads to eye strain and other physical problems. EyeLeo guarantees you won't forget to take a break.
